Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding information when you use the AwesomeCopy Mobile app.

Information We Collect

The AwesomeCopy Mobile app itself does not collect or store any personal information directly from its users. All clipboard data managed by the app remains stored locally on your device and in your personal iCloud account, and is never transmitted to us or any third party.

We do not require you to provide any personal data to use the core features of the app.

For subscription management, we use RevenueCat, a third-party service that processes purchase information. RevenueCat may collect certain identifying information such as user IDs, device information, and transaction details to manage your subscription status. This information is used solely for managing your subscription and providing access to premium features. For more information about how RevenueCat handles your data, please review their Privacy Policy.

iCloud Sync

AwesomeCopy Mobile uses iCloud to sync your clipboard history between your iOS devices and Mac. This data is stored in your personal iCloud account and is protected by Apple's security measures. We do not have access to this data, and it is not transmitted to our servers.

Data Usage

Since we do not collect personal information directly, we do not use it.

Any diagnostic or crash report data collected by Apple through the standard App Store mechanisms is anonymous and used solely for improving app stability and performance.

Information collected by RevenueCat is used exclusively for subscription management purposes, including verifying purchase status, providing access to premium features, and processing transactions. This data is handled according to RevenueCat's privacy policy and is necessary for the functioning of subscription features.

Data Security

Your clipboard data is secured using Apple's built-in iOS security features and iCloud encryption. We do not have access to your clipboard history or any personal data stored in the app.

Children's Privacy

AwesomeCopy Mobile is not specifically designed for children. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from any users, including children.

Third-Party Services

AwesomeCopy Mobile uses the following third-party services:

  • RevenueCat: For managing in-app subscriptions. RevenueCat collects and processes transaction information and device identifiers necessary for subscription management. This information is governed by RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.
  • iCloud: For syncing clipboard data between your devices. This data is stored in your personal iCloud account and is subject to Apple's privacy policies.
  • Apple Services: Standard Apple services for crash reporting and analytics may collect anonymized data as per Apple's policies.

Your Rights

Since all your data is stored locally on your device or in your personal iCloud account, you have complete control over it. You can delete the app at any time to remove all local data, and manage your iCloud data through your device settings.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at appspire@icloud.com.
