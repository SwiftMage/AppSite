Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2024

Thank you for choosing Paycheck - Salary Calculator. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

Paycheck - Salary Calculator is designed with your privacy in mind. We collect minimal personal information:

  • User Preferences: We store your salary settings, hourly rates, and tax settings locally on your device to provide a personalized experience.
  • Usage Data: Anonymous data about how you use the app to help us improve functionality and user experience.

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our service
  • To improve the app's functionality and user experience
  • To personalize your experience by saving your settings
  • To analyze usage patterns and optimize performance

Data Storage

Your salary, tax, and personal settings are stored locally on your device. We do not store this information on our servers.

Third-Party Services

We use RevenueCat for subscription management and in-app purchases. RevenueCat collects and processes data according to their privacy policy. By using our app, you also agree to the RevenueCat Privacy Policy.

RevenueCat may collect certain information, including:

  • Purchase history
  • Subscription status

Analytics

We may use analytics tools to collect anonymous information about how you use our app. This data helps us improve the app's functionality and user experience.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • The right to access the personal information we hold about you
  • The right to request correction of inaccurate information
  • The right to request deletion of your information
  • The right to restrict or object to processing

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
