Features

  • Lives in your menu bar for easy access
  • Tracks clipboard history (configurable limit)
  • Shows copy timestamps
  • Click to re-copy items
  • Pin important items
  • Beautiful "Copy Stack" window for files
  • Comprehensive settings
  • Automatic updates
  • Privacy-focused: All data stays local

Why Awesome Copy?

Awesome Copy is a powerful macOS menu bar app designed to supercharge your workflow. It elegantly manages your clipboard history, allowing quick access, pinning, and seamless re-copying. The unique "Copy Stack" feature provides a visually stunning way to handle copied files. With extensive customization and a focus on privacy, Awesome Copy is the clipboard manager you've been waiting for.
