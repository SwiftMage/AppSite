Privacy Policy

Last Updated: September 2024

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding information when you use the Awesome Copy app.

Information We Collect

The Awesome Copy app itself **does not collect or store any personal information** directly from its users. All clipboard data managed by the app remains stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party.

We do not require you to provide any personal data to use the core features of the app.

If we introduce features requiring purchases or subscriptions in the future, this policy will be updated to detail any necessary data handling, potentially involving third-party services like RevenueCat for purchase management, similar to our other apps. Currently, no such data is collected.

Data Usage

Since we do not collect personal information directly, we do not use it.

Any diagnostic or crash report data collected by Apple through the standard App Store mechanisms is anonymous and used solely for improving app stability and performance.

Children's Privacy

Awesome Copy is not specifically designed for children. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from any users, including children.

Third-Party Services

Currently, Awesome Copy does not rely on third-party services that collect personal user data for its core functionality. Standard Apple services for crash reporting and analytics may collect anonymized data as per Apple's policies.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at appspire@icloud.com.
